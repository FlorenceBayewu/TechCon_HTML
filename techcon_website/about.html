History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local technologists passionate about open-source software. Over the years, it has grown into a major international event, evolving to cover topics from AI and machine learning to sustainable technology and digital ethics. We are proud of our journey from a community meetup to a global thought leadership platform.

Our Mission

Our core mission is to **inspire innovation**, **foster connection**, and **drive change** within the global technology community. We strive to create an inclusive environment where industry leaders, developers, and students can share knowledge and collaborate on the challenges of tomorrow.

Past Speakers Hall of Fame

We have been honored to host some of the most influential figures in the technology landscape. Here are a few notable speakers from previous years:

Dr. Anya Sharma (2022)

Dr. Sharma is a pioneer in ethical AI development and led a groundbreaking session on **Algorithmic Fairness**. Her contributions continue to shape responsible innovation.

Kenji Tanaka (2018)

Tanaka revolutionized cloud architecture with his keynote on **Serverless Computing at Scale**, a talk frequently cited as a turning point in the industry.
